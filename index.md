## Key Features

### Instant Transactions
Transactions confirm in **300-600 milliseconds** with finality - no waiting, no block times, no reorganizations.

### Zero Fees
**Truly feeless** - send any amount without paying transaction fees. Perfect for micropayments, tips, and everyday transactions.

### Eco-Friendly
No energy-intensive mining for consensus. Atto uses **Open Representative Voting (ORV)**, consuming power comparable to just six average households for the entire network.

### Secure & Decentralized
Each account maintains its own transaction chain. Representatives vote on transactions with delegated voting weight, achieving fast finality without centralization.

### Scalable
Account-chain architecture allows the network to process transactions in parallel, with no global bottlenecks.

## How Atto Works

### Open Representative Voting (ORV)
Instead of mining blocks, Atto holders **delegate their voting weight** to representatives they trust. These representatives vote on the validity of transactions, achieving consensus quickly and efficiently.

### Account Chains
Unlike traditional blockchains with a single global chain, **each Atto account has its own chain** of transactions. This allows parallel processing and eliminates the need for miners.

### Anti-Spam Protection
To prevent spam without fees, Atto requires a small amount of **Proof-of-Work** for each transaction. This PoW is quick to compute on modern devices but makes large-scale spam attacks economically infeasible.

### Fixed Supply
**18 billion Atto tokens** were created at genesis - no new tokens will ever be minted. All tokens are distributed through community programs, ensuring fair access.
